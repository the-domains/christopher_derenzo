Christopher DeRenzo / Professional Blog

This was a motorized piece of furniture with the A/V equipment concealed behind the doors and a motorized television lift. Mr. Huskey requested a “classic” design that blended with the existing bedroom furniture. The intention was to conceal all of the components fashionably and have a motor move the television up through the counter top by the push of a button.

Constructed with Birch Plywood and solid maple top and edges

Finishing the rough work and prepping for the stain.

First coat of stain was not dark enough for the customer so I began the next coat

Bass wood and hand tools were used to carve the corbels

the inside without the countertop

Routed edges to create a beautiful accent to the counter top

Everything fit perfectly
